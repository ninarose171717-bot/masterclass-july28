Your Host

Nina Rose

I created this space for the woman who is yearning to soften. To stop performing self-love and start noticing herself, … and maybe even allow herself to feel an exhale in that. For the woman who wants to slowly begin to open her heart … to herself first.

This is the space for the woman who wants to gather and share, without overthinking, without the fear of being judged. Who might find her closest friends here or discover what she doesn’t want, and feel free to say no. Who is starting to notice herself, in herself and in other women, … and stay.

In the past few years, I’ve worked with women online and in person, as well as with people in recovery in rehab, around one pattern that sits beneath so much of what keeps us stuck: the habit of leaving ourselves.

My work is grounded in the body, because knowing something isn’t the same as feeling ourselves in it. More than anything, what I bring to this work is the practice of staying with ourselves, without rushing to fix, change, or numb.

I created this community because I was missing a place like this. A cozy little corner for just us, raw and rare and beautiful. Where we could all show up openly and vulnerably, as much as the trust allows.
